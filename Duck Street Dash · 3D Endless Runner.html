Street-ready, duck?

Dodge cars, hop over water, and survive as long as you can.

Keyboard: ← / → to lane switch · ↑ to jump

Touch controls

Big buttons for thumbs. You can also swipe up to jump.

How to play

  • 🏎️ Cars will try to cut you off. If they hit you, the run ends.
  • 🌊 Streets are broken by canals. Miss the road and you splash out.
  • ⚡ Survive longer to ramp up the speed and your score multiplier.
  • 🛡️ Level 2 only: grab glowing orbs to gain a one-hit shield against cars.
  • 💾 Best score is saved locally in this browser.
